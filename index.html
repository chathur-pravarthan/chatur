About Us

Chathur Pravarthan Private Limited is a technology-driven startup that emerged from the prestigious New Age Innovation Network (NAIN 1.0) initiative, funded by the Government of Karnataka (GoK). Incubated at ME-RIISE FOUNDATION, Malnad College of Engineering, Hassan, our company is committed to developing smart and sustainable solutions across multiple domains, including renewable energy, agriculture, and healthcare.

Founded on November 22, 2022, our vision is to create impactful, technology-driven solutions that address real-world challenges while fostering innovation and entrepreneurship in the ecosystem.

Our Innovation Focus Areas

Chathur Pravarthan specializes in IoT-based automation and AI-driven smart systems. Our flagship innovation, the Automated Solar Power Cleaning Robot, provides a cutting-edge solution for the maintenance and cleaning of solar panels, optimizing energy generation and ensuring long-term efficiency.

In addition, we are actively working on the design and development of low-cost imaging modality systems to enhance accessibility and affordability in the healthcare sector. Our research and development efforts extend to agri-tech solutions, where we are developing automated irrigation systems with real-time monitoring to improve efficiency and yield in agricultural practices.

Solar panel cleaning robots

Renewable Energy

Advanced solar panel cleaning systems for optimized energy efficiency and sustainable power generation.

Agri-Tech Solutions

Smart irrigation systems with soil moisture sensors and weather-based automation for precision farming.

Healthcare Technology

Affordable AI-powered imaging systems reducing diagnostic costs by 40% while maintaining clinical accuracy.
